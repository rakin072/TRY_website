About TRY

TRY, KUET-এর একটি স্বেচ্ছাসেবী সংগঠন, মানবিক সহায়তা, শিক্ষা, স্বাস্থ্যসেবা এবং দুর্যোগকালীন সহায়তায় নিরলসভাবে কাজ করে যাচ্ছে।

We are a student-led humanitarian platform at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology, driven by empathy, service, and collective action.

Mission Compassion-first service
Vision Dignity and opportunity for all
Values Integrity, Inclusion, Unity

What We Do

Emergency Fund Collection

Rapid fundraising drives to support urgent community needs.

Education Support

Mentoring, materials, and support for learners in need.

Disaster Relief

Coordinated aid for flood, storm, and crisis-affected families.

Food Distribution

Community meal drives for vulnerable neighborhoods.

Medical Aid

Health camps, medicine support, and awareness outreach.

Community Development

Long-term local projects built with community participation.
